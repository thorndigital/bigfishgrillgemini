A Message to Our Valued Guests

Thank you Issaquah! With sincere gratitude, we thank you for a wonderful 10 years! It is with a heavy heart that we said good-bye to our Issaquah Highlands location on March 31st. We've celebrated countless birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays with you. These are memories our family and team will cherish forever.

We are thrilled to continue serving your favorite Big Fish Grill dishes at our Woodinville location. We remain open for lunch and dinner daily and look forward to welcoming you!

Sincerely,
The Orrico Family
and all of us at Big Fish Grill
