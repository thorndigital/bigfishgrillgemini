A Message to Our Valued Guests

Thank you, Issaquah!

With sincere gratitude, we thank you for a wonderful 10 years! It is with a heavy heart that we announce the closure of our Issaquah Highlands location as of March 31st. We have cherished the memories of serving thousands of guests and celebrating countless birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays with you.

We hope you will continue to enjoy your favorite Big Fish Grill dishes at our Woodinville location, which remains open and ready to welcome you for lunch and dinner daily. We look forward to seeing you there!

Sincerely,
The Orrico Family & The Big Fish Grill Team
