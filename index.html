A Message to Our Valued Guests

Thank you Issaquah! With sincere gratitude, we thank you all for a wonderful 10 years! It is with a heavy heart that it's time to say good-bye. The Big Fish Grill in the Issaquah Highlands closed on Friday, March 31st. We have served thousands of guests, celebrated birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and more. These are memories that our family and team will be forever grateful for.

We hope you will continue to enjoy your favorite Big Fish Grill menu items at our **Woodinville location**. The Woodinville Big Fish Grill remains open and serves lunch and dinner daily. We look forward to seeing you there!

Sincerely,
The Orrico family and all of us at Big Fish Grill
