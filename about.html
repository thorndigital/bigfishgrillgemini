Our Story

From our family's passion to your plate—discover the journey of Big Fish Grill.

Rooted in Passion for Fresh Seafood

The Big Fish Grill story began with the Orrico family's deep-seated love for fresh, quality seafood and a desire to create a welcoming place where friends and families could gather. For years, we've poured our hearts into perfecting our recipes, sourcing the finest ingredients, and cultivating an atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.

A New Chapter in Woodinville

While our chapter in Issaquah has concluded, our dedication to serving exceptional seafood with genuine hospitality remains stronger than ever at our beloved Woodinville location. This vibrant community has embraced us, and we are proud to continue our tradition here.

Every dish is a testament to our commitment: from classic Fish & Chips to innovative seasonal specials, each plate is crafted with care, emphasizing the fresh, local flavors of the Pacific Northwest.

Our Commitment to You

We are more than just a restaurant; we are a part of the Woodinville fabric. We are committed to supporting local purveyors and contributing to our community however we can. We invite you to experience the Big Fish Grill difference—where delicious food, a friendly ambiance, and lasting memories are always on the menu.
